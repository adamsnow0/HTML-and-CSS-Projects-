Hilary Hahn

Hilary Hahn, born November 27, 1979, is an American violinist, a three-time Grammy Award winner, and she has performed throughout the world as a soloist with leading orchestras and conductors, and as a recitalist.

The accompanying video (at the link above) is of a performance she did on 7 September 2023, alongside the Frankfurt Radio Symphony, of the Violin Concerto in D major, Op 35 by Classical composer Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikivsky.
